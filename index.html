How to Convert CGPA to Percentage?

Most Indian universities follow this standard formula:

Percentage = CGPA × 9.5

For example, if your CGPA is 8.2:

8.2 × 9.5 = 77.9%

Why Do Universities Use CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is used to standardize grading across semesters and subjects. It provides a uniform evaluation system for students.

Important Note

Some universities use different formulas. Always verify with your institution’s official guidelines.

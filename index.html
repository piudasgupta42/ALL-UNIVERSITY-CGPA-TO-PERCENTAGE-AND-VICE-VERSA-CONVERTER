How CGPA to Percentage is Calculated?

Different Indian universities follow different conversion formulas. Most common formula is:

Percentage = CGPA × 9.5

However, some universities apply different multipliers or additional constants.

Important Note

Always verify the official conversion method from your university before submitting documents for jobs or higher studies.
